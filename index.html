Who We Are

The Story Behind
Kriti Design Studio

Founded in 2010 by Kriti Sharma, our studio began as a small atelier in the walled city of Jaipur. Inspired by the rich textile traditions of Rajasthan, Kriti set out to create a space where heritage and modernity could coexist beautifully.

What started as a passion project has grown into a celebrated boutique serving brides, professionals, and fashion-forward women across India. Every garment we create reflects our deep respect for craftsmanship and our commitment to celebrating the modern Indian woman.

Our team of 20+ skilled artisans, embroiderers, and designers work tirelessly to ensure that each piece meets our exacting standards of quality and elegance.

Shobha jaishwal
Founder & Head Designer

Shipping Policy

At Kriti Design Studio, we strive to deliver your bespoke and ready-to-wear collections with the utmost care and efficiency. All domestic orders are processed and shipped within 3 to 5 business days. Custom and bridal orders require additional processing time as specified during the consultation.

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via email. Delivery times vary by location, but typically range from 2 to 7 business days within the country. International shipping is subject to longer transit times and potential customs delays.

Please note that shipping times are estimates and not guaranteed. We are not responsible for delays caused by the carrier or customs clearance.

Return Policy

We want you to love your purchase. If for any reason you are not completely satisfied with a ready-to-wear item, you may return it within 14 days of receipt for an exchange or store credit. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and have all original tags attached.

Custom and Bridal Orders: Due to the personalized nature of these garments, we do not accept returns or exchanges on custom-made or altered pieces. All sales are final on these items.

To initiate a return, please contact our support team. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item received was defective or incorrect.

Privacy Policy

Care Instructions

Our garments are crafted from premium fabrics and feature intricate hand-embroidery. To ensure their longevity and maintain their beauty, please follow these care instructions carefully.

Dry Clean Only: We strongly recommend professional dry cleaning for all silk, organza, and heavily embroidered items. Do not machine wash or hand wash garments with zari work or delicate embellishments.

Store your garments in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Use breathable cotton or muslin garment bags to protect them from dust and moisture. Avoid spraying perfume directly onto the fabric, especially on zari or metallic embroidery, as it may cause tarnishing.
